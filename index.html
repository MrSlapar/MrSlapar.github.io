Welcome to Ana, Your Biograd Buddy

Your personal assistant for exploring Biograd. To test the demo, click the chat icon in the bottom right corner to start.

Please note that this version of Ana AI is for demonstration purposes only. The information provided is not validated and does not draw from a comprehensive knowledge base. For production purposes, the AI will be equipped with all the necessary information, documents, PDFs, presentations, recommendations, and up-to-date data that you wish to communicate.

The current integration method, displayed as a pop-up on this website, is also for demonstration purposes. The integration can be customized to different platforms such as WhatsApp, a dedicated web application, Facebook Messenger, and others, according to your preferences and needs.

The language selection at the beginning of the conversation is also for demonstration purposes, showing that Ana can communicate in multiple languages. In the production version, Ana can continue the entire conversation in the language of the first question posed, supporting over 50 different languages.

Thank you for your understanding.
